CodingZone Mission Statement

The Coding Zone is a student and faculty driven project with a mission to provide website services and training, as a community service, to the Northeast Ohio small business community. The intent is to produce high quality websites, and corresponding services, at reasonable prices while helping students pay for their college education.

The core premise of the Coding Zone is for students to provide solid products and services, interact with the local community, gain invaluable experience for the professional portfolio and have fun as they indulge their passion for programming.
